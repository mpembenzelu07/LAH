Eligibility Information

Our free legal services are available to individuals and families who meet income eligibility requirements. We prioritize assistance for:

  • Low-income individuals and families
  • Innovators and entrepreneurs from underserved communities
  • Farmers and agricultural workers
  • Artisans and traditional craft workers
  • Individuals facing urgent legal crises

If you're unsure whether you qualify, please still submit a request. We review all applications and may be able to refer you to other resources if we cannot assist directly.

For Lawyers Wanting to Volunteer
