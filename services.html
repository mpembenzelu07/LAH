Our Legal Services

Comprehensive free legal assistance for innovators, farmers, artisans, and underserved communities

How We Help

LEGAL-AID HUB provides a wide range of free legal services tailored to the specific needs of underserved communities. Our volunteer lawyers offer expert guidance in multiple areas of law.

1

Consultation

Free initial consultation to understand your legal needs

2

Assessment

Our legal team assesses your case and determines the best approach

3

Assistance

We provide the legal help you need, from advice to representation

4

Follow-up

We ensure you have ongoing support throughout your legal journey

Business Legal Help for Innovators

We support educated innovators and entrepreneurs starting businesses with:

  • Business Formation: Help with registering LLCs, corporations, partnerships
  • Contract Drafting & Review: Client agreements, vendor contracts, partnership agreements
  • Intellectual Property: Trademark registration, copyright protection, patent guidance
  • Regulatory Compliance: Navigating business regulations and licenses
  • Employment Law: Drafting employment contracts and understanding labor laws

Who qualifies: Entrepreneurs with limited financial resources, startups in early stages, innovators from underserved communities.

Farmers & Land Rights Protection

We help farming communities secure and protect their most valuable asset: land.

  • Land Title Assistance: Help securing and verifying land titles
  • Agricultural Contracts: Reviewing and drafting farming agreements
  • Dispute Resolution: Mediation for land boundary and ownership disputes
  • Inheritance Planning: Ensuring land passes correctly to next generations
  • Government Program Navigation: Assistance with agricultural subsidies and programs

Who qualifies: Small-scale farmers, agricultural workers, farming families facing land disputes.

Artisan Support & Protection

We empower artisans to protect their creative works and build sustainable businesses.

  • Copyright Protection: Securing rights to traditional designs and crafts
  • Fair Trade Agreements: Reviewing contracts with retailers and exporters
  • Business Formation: Help establishing cooperatives or small businesses
  • Contract Review: Ensuring fair terms in exhibition and sales agreements
  • Intellectual Property Education: Workshops on protecting creative works

Who qualifies: Traditional artisans, craft workers, cultural heritage practitioners.

Housing & Tenancy Rights

We protect tenants and homeowners facing housing challenges.

  • Eviction Defense: Legal representation for wrongful evictions
  • Lease Review: Analysis of rental agreements for fairness
  • Housing Discrimination: Addressing discriminatory housing practices
  • Foreclosure Prevention: Assistance for homeowners at risk of foreclosure
  • Repair & Habitability Issues: Ensuring landlords meet legal obligations

Family Law Assistance

We provide support for family-related legal matters.

  • Divorce & Separation: Guidance through the legal process
  • Child Custody & Support: Protecting children's best interests
  • Domestic Violence Protection: Help obtaining restraining orders
  • Adoption Assistance: Guidance through adoption procedures
  • Guardianship: Establishing legal guardianship when needed

Ready to Get Help?

If you need legal assistance and meet our eligibility criteria, we're here to help. Our services are completely free for qualifying individuals and communities.
